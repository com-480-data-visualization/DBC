Where is happiness?

Happiness is not evenly distributed across the globe. Northern Europe consistently leads, while Sub-Saharan Africa faces the steepest challenges. Explore the map to see how scores vary by country and year.

Part 2

What drives happiness?

Happiness scores are built from six factors: GDP, social support, health, freedom, generosity, and trust in government. Their relative weight varies dramatically across regions. Select a region to compare.

Part 3

When did things change?

While the global average has barely moved, individual countries have experienced dramatic shifts. Venezuela plunged nearly two points; Benin rose over one and a half. Click countries on the chart to compare their trajectories.
